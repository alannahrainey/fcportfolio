Reflection

This is an experimental clock design created in p5.js. The clock represents the passing of time through the rotting of an apple. The apple is a 3D sphere that progressively rots. To create a dynamic composition, I used rotate features to show the dimensions of the apple as it rots. For the clock that is positioned in the top left corner, I used lines with angles tied to seconds, minutes, and hours. I learned how to use new aspects of JavaScript in this assignment such as cos(), sin(), map, and lerp.
